Art is a contradiction.

Art is everywhere and nowhere.

We have no idea what it is

But we know it when we see it.

Art is invaluable and worthless.

Art is a $120,000 banana.

Making art is not a real job.

No one wants to be an artist, but

Everyone wants what they have.

We can't live without art

But no one can afford it.

Art is Not A Luxury
