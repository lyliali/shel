Tip Jar

Nothing in life is free,

but that doesn't mean you have to pay for it.

There are many ways to express gratitude,

and I believe in the emergence

of new ways of exchanging value

like the gift economy.

A kind word or heartfelt recommendation

on my behalf

goes a long way.

But

there's also a good old fashioned tip jar. :)
